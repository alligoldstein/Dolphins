Bottlenose Dolphins

Fun Facts:

  • They have two stomachs, one for digestion and one for storage
  • They can live up to fifty years
  • Dolphins have to tell themselves when to breathe, it is not automatic like it is for humans
  • India, Hungary, Costa Rica, and Chile have declared dolphins "non-human persons," meaning that they can't be captured and used for entertainment purposes, for example, in aquariums
